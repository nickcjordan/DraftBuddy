What is fantasy football ADP?
ADP represents the Average Draft Position for players in fantasy football drafts. It serves as a useful draft prep tool for understanding how players are valued. Our ADP Composite consists of consensus draft values across the most popular league hosts.
* Data based on real early season drafts may be from a small sample size.

ESPN ADP can be found under PPR scoring. Yahoo ADP can be found under Half PPR scoring.
