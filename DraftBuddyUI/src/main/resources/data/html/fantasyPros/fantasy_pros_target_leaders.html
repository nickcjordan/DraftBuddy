What are NFL targets?
Targets represent the number of times a player has had a pass thrown his way, regardless of whether a catch was made. Highly targeted players offer greater upside, especially in Point Per Reception (PPR) leagues, since each target is an opportunity to accumulate a reception, touchdown and receiving yards.
